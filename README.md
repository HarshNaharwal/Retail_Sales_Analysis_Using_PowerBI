# 🛒 Retail Sales Analytics Dashboard | Power BI

## 📌 1. Introduction

This project was developed for **NovaMart Retail Group**, a fictional retail company operating multiple stores across different regions.

The goal of this project was to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer behavior, product performance, and regional trends using **Power BI**.

Using a star schema data model and interactive dashboards, this project helps management understand business performance and make data-driven decisions.

---

## ❓ 2. Business Problem

NovaMart Retail Group noticed several business challenges:

* Sales growth was slowing down.
* Profit margins were decreasing.
* Some regions were underperforming.
* Discounts were impacting profitability.
* Management lacked a centralized view of business performance.

The leadership team wanted to understand the root causes of these issues and identify opportunities for growth.

---

## 🎯 3. Goal of the Dashboard

The main goals of this dashboard are to:

* Monitor overall sales and profit performance.
* Identify top-performing and underperforming products.
* Analyze customer purchasing behavior.
* Compare performance across regions and stores.
* Track important KPIs for business decision-making.
* Provide actionable recommendations to improve profitability and growth.

### Key KPIs

* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Profit Margin %
* Total Orders
* Total Customers
* Average Order Value (AOV)

---

## 📊 4. Dashboard Walkthrough

### Page 1: Executive Overview

This page provides a high-level summary of business performance.

#### Visuals

* KPI Cards (Sales, Profit, Orders, Customers, Profit Margin)
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales by Region
* Sales by Category
* Top 10 Products by Sales

#### Purpose

Allows management to quickly understand overall business performance.

---

### Page 2: Sales & Product Analysis

This page focuses on product and category performance.

#### Visuals

* Sales by Category
* Profit by Category
* Top 10 Products
* Bottom 10 Products
* Discount vs Profit Analysis

#### Purpose

Helps identify profitable products and products that need improvement.

---

### Page 3: Customer & Regional Analysis

This page focuses on customer behavior and regional performance.

#### Visuals

* Sales by Customer Segment
* Sales by Age Group
* Profit by Region
* Store Performance Analysis
* Customer Distribution

#### Purpose

Helps understand customer value and regional business performance.

---

## 🔍 5. Key Insights

### Sales Insights

* Electronics generated the highest sales revenue.
* A small number of products contributed a large portion of total revenue.
* Sales increased during festive months.

### Profit Insights

* Some products generated high sales but low profit.
* Higher discounts reduced overall profitability.
* Profit performance varied significantly across categories.

### Customer Insights

* VIP customers generated higher revenue per customer.
* Premium customers contributed strongly to profit.
* Customer purchasing behavior differed across segments.

### Regional Insights

* Certain regions outperformed others in both sales and profit.
* Some stores consistently delivered stronger results.
* Regional performance gaps highlighted opportunities for improvement.
